FAQ
When will Simplecoin be listed?
We are actively negotiating with leading platforms. Follow our updates on social media and channels to ensure you don't miss any important information. More detailed information will be available later.
Why is Simplecoin withdrawal available only to those with more than 3 referrals?
These limits are designed to optimize the coin accumulation process in the Simple app. At present, we offer withdrawal options only to our most active users. We plan to lower this requirement in the future to allow more users to benefit.
If I can't withdraw coins from Simple Coin, will they be lost?
No, your coins will remain secure in the Simple Coin app. There are no limits on how long you can store them, and you can continue to accumulate or use them to enhance your profile.
Why can only 10% of the coin balance be withdrawn?
This restriction is in place to ensure that coins are always available for you to develop your profile.
Can I return coins from the Simple app to Simple Coin?
At the initial stage, it is not possible to return withdrawn coins. The function to return coins from the Simple app to the Simple Coin application will be implemented later, so your coins will not be lost.
